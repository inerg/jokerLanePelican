About

How long have you been racing RC Cars?

I have been racing for about 4 years now, since 2011.

What is your favorite thing about RC Racing?

I really like the racing. Main events where the pressure is on and it's all on the line. But I also really like the community. Most guys are willing to give a helping hand to other racers, parts and other tips they may need. It's nice to see people not letting competitiveness get in the way always.

How would you describe your driving style?

Smooth but punched. My number one goal is to not crash going around the track. But I like to push how fast I can drive my car around sweepers and coming up to jumps.

What is your favorite class to drive?

My favourite class to run is 1/8 buggy. It changes on the day whether it's electric or nitro.

What are your future goals in RC Racing?

I am and always will be about the fun of the hobby. Although one day it would be very cool to compete in a national or even world event. For now I'm just focusing on doing well at a local level.
